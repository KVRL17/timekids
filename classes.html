School Curriculum

In Our Curriculam, We Develop The below Goals

Social Skills
Cognitive Skills
Emotional Skills
Physical Skills
Language Development
Literacy Development
Art and Craft
Creative skills

At TimeKids Preschool, we foster social skills by encouraging children to interact, share, and collaborate with their peers in a safe, structured environment. Through group activities, games, and play, kids learn the importance of cooperation, respect, and empathy, building the foundations for positive relationships and teamwork.

Our preschool curriculum is designed to enhance cognitive skills by stimulating children’s curiosity and critical thinking. Engaging activities like puzzles, problem-solving games, and hands-on learning experiences help to develop reasoning, memory, and the ability to process information, ensuring a strong intellectual foundation.

We nurture emotional development by creating a supportive and caring environment where children can express their feelings and learn to understand the emotions of others. By teaching self-regulation, resilience, and empathy through storytelling and role-playing, we help kids develop confidence and emotional intelligence.

At TimeKids Preschool, we promote physical development through a range of activities that enhance both gross and fine motor skills. From outdoor play, running, and jumping to arts, crafts, and building blocks, children are encouraged to improve their coordination, strength, and overall physical well-being.

We focus on language development by providing opportunities for children to expand their vocabulary, improve their communication, and practice verbal expression. Through storytelling, singing, and interactive conversations, we help kids build a strong foundation for effective speaking and listening skills.

Our literacy program introduces children to the world of reading and writing through fun and engaging activities. By incorporating phonics, letter recognition, and early writing exercises, we help preschoolers develop the skills they need to begin reading and writing confidently, setting the stage for future academic success.

TimeKids Preschool encourages creativity and self-expression through a variety of art and craft activities. By using different materials, colors, and techniques, children explore their imagination and develop fine motor skills, all while having fun creating something uniquely their own.

We believe that creativity is a vital part of a child’s development, and we cultivate this through imaginative play, music, dance, and creative storytelling. Encouraging kids to think outside the box and explore new ideas helps them develop problem-solving abilities, innovation, and a love for learning.
